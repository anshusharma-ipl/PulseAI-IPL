1. Introduction

In the highly competitive enterprise B2B SaaS market, retaining high-value accounts is the foundation of sustainable growth. Today, IPL launched Pulse AI, a Generative AI-powered Customer Health Intelligence platform that unifies disparate account signals into a single, explainable system of action.

By consolidating behavioral, billing, and sentiment data, Pulse AI equips customer-facing teams with the predictive clarity needed to prevent churn before it occurs. This strategic launch enables SaaS organizations to protect millions in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) and shift from reactive crisis management to proactive revenue protection.

2. Executive Summary

Enterprise Customer Success (CS) teams have long struggled with fragmented customer data scattered across CRMs, product analytics, support tickets, and billing portals. Pulse AI solves this challenge by acting as an intelligent decision layer that automatically ingests and correlates these multi-system signals.

Utilizing an advanced, explainable Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture, the platform replaces vague health scores with clear, root-cause analyses and stakeholder-specific "next-best-action" recommendations. This transforms how organizations defend and expand their revenue, delivering high-impact outcomes including reduced churn, accelerated issue resolution, and streamlined cross-functional alignment. Pulse AI differentiates itself by tying product usage and customer friction directly to revenue-at-risk, allowing teams to prioritize their efforts based on true business impact.

3. Core Industry Problems

Problem 1

Siloed and Fragmented Customer Data

Customer signals are scattered across disconnected systems (CRM, product analytics, Zendesk, billing, and error logs). Because there is no single source of truth, organizations miss critical early warning signs of account distress, leading to unexpected, high-value churn that directly damages quarterly bookings.

Problem 2

Vague, Opaque Health Scores

Traditional CS platforms generate generic "red/yellow/green" health indicators without context. Without knowing why an account is flagged, Customer Success Managers (CSMs) must spend hours manually digging through support tickets and usage metrics, delaying critical outreach until the customer has already decided to leave.

Problem 3

Misaligned Cross-Functional Prioritization

Product and Engineering teams build roadmaps and fix bugs based on technical severity or gut feel rather than business impact. Because they lack visibility into how specific technical glitches affect high-ARR accounts, critical revenue-impacting issues remain unresolved while low-impact tasks are prioritized.

4. The Solution

Pulse AI acts as a unified decision platform that addresses these pain points by consolidating scattered data into real-time, explainable intelligence.

Value to Customer Success

Replaces hours of manual weekly report generation with instant, plain-English account summaries and ready-to-send outreach plans, increasing team productivity.

Value to Product & Engineering

Ranks open bugs, feature requests, and system errors by the total ARR of the affected accounts, directly aligning engineering focus with revenue.
